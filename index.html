About Us

Innovative Way To Learn

Signal Village National High School (SVNHS) is a premier public high school in Taguig City, serving as a cornerstone for secondary education in the community since 1976. With a mission to provide quality education, the school offers a well-rounded curriculum combining academic excellence with technical-vocational training to prepare students for higher education and employment. Equipped with modern facilities like air-conditioned classrooms, a cyber lab, and counseling support, SVNHS fosters a nurturing environment for over 9,000 students annually. The school emphasizes innovation, community involvement, and the holistic development of learners, empowering them to excel academically and contribute positively to society.
