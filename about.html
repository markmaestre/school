About Us

Innovative Way To Learn

Signal Village National High School (SVNHS) is a leading public secondary school in Taguig City, established in 1976. Over the decades, SVNHS has grown into a vibrant educational institution, catering to over 9,000 students annually, making it one of the largest high schools in the area. The school is committed to fostering academic excellence, character development, and community engagement through a dynamic and inclusive learning environment.

More

Our Mission

To provide quality and accessible education, ensuring that every learner is equipped with the knowledge, skills, and values needed to succeed in higher education, careers, and life.

  • Our Vision

    • A school that nurtures globally competitive and responsible individuals who contribute positively to their communities.

  • Community Engagement

    • SVNHS prides itself on being a cornerstone of the Signal Village community. Through partnerships with local stakeholders, it runs various programs promoting road safety, environmental awareness, and youth leadership.

  • Equipped with modern facilities like air-conditioned classrooms, a cyber lab, and counseling support, SVNHS fosters a nurturing environment for over 9,000 students annually. The school emphasizes innovation, community involvement, and the holistic development of learners, empowering them to excel academically and contribute positively to society.
