HUMSS Strand at Signal Village National High School

OVERVIEW

The Humanities and Social Sciences (HUMSS) strand at Signal Village National High School (SVNHS) is designed for students interested in pursuing careers related to social sciences, communication, education, and public service. HUMSS focuses on honing critical thinking, empathy, and communication skills, empowering students to analyze human behavior, society, and culture while preparing them for leadership roles in diverse fields.
